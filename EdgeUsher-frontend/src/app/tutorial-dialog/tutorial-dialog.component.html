Tutorial

  • Overview: In the header you can specify a title from the chain, set a max latency constraint, execute EdgeUsher or open the settings menu to reset, download or upload a chain file or a chain project (same upload button). In the left side you can navigate the view of the application through the dots. At the cente there is the drawing area to draw the chain. In the bottom of the page there are buttons to change the view mode for this chain or to view chain and infrastructure together. If you hover with the mouse on an icon or a button you can see what it do.

  • Step 1: to insert a service click on the drawing area and specify the service's requirements.

  • Step 2: to insert a flow click on a service and drag the mouse over another service, then specify the flow's requirements.
  • Step 3: to insert a max latency constraint click on the button on the header, then click on a service to add it to the max latency constraint.

  • Step 4: to modify a component of the chain click on it.

  • Step 5: to start EdgeUsher click on the play icon on the header, next specify the placement requested or the affinity/anti-affinity constraint then click "submit". You can also request an heuristic execution of the program adding the two thresholds.

  • Overview: In the header you can select a placement between those returned by EdgeUsher, open the settings menu to reset all or download a file that represent the selected placement or execute EdgeUsher. In the left side you can navigate the view of the application through the dots. At the cente there is the drawing area to customize the placement. In the bottom of the page there are buttons to change the view mode for this placement (source code or graphic editor). If you hover with the mouse on an icon or a button you can see what it do.

  • Step 1: select a placement in the menu at the top-left corner of the page.

  • Step 2: to customize the placement selected click on a service and drag the service on another node or on the unplaced area, then drop it.

  • Step 3: if you have modified the placement you can run EdheUsher to evaluate it by clicking on the play button on the header, next specify the affinity or anti-affinity constraint for the unplaced services or set the threshold for heuristic version (both optional) then click submit and wait for the result.
